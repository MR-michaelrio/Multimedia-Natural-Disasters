STORM

Hurricanes, typhoons, and cyclones are all types of tropical storms with the same composition and characteristics. Their main difference is their geographical location. Tropical storms in South Pacific and Indian Oceans are called Cyclones , in Northwest Pacific Ocean they are called Typhoons, and in North Atlantic and Northeast Pacific Oceans tropical storms are called Hurricanes.

Frequency from 2000 - 2023
Impact to Communities

People are injured or killed by debris being blown around

Bringing rain to dry areas and moving heat from the tropics poleward.

Water supplies become contaminated. A lack of clean water and water-borne diseases can affect local people.

Damage to roads and nearby infrastructure.

Economic disruption caused by the damages

Power outage caused by the hurricane’s path.
